GoBean makes single-use packaging sustainable. Used coffee grounds are collected from cafés and pressed into 100% compostable cup sleeves — locally collected, ergonomically designed, part of the circular economy.

The result

The GoBean Sleeve.

Heat- and water-resistant. Ergonomically shaped. Fully compostable within 3–4 weeks. Plant it and see seedlings within a week.

Circular Economy

From cradle to cradle.

Cafés supply grounds. Receive a discount. Customers get a sustainable sleeve. Everyone benefits. The loop closes.
